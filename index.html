Why?

Quite simply - because it was a meetup we wanted to attend to. We're all enthusiasts of developing on Apple platforms and we wanted a space where we could discuss recent developments with fellow-minded developers.

For whom?

For everyone! We aim to cover a wide range of topics - from basic, introductory talks to more technical ones that delve into the nitty-gritty details. If you're interested in Swift in any shape or form - you're invited to join us! You may also want to take a look at guide.
